Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

CV Maker (“the App”) lets you sign in with Google, create a CV, and save it to your Google account. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we access

  • Google account info (name, email, profile picture) — to identify you when you sign in.
  • Google Drive — to create and update one file (cv-maker.json) that this App saves for you. We only access files created by this App. We do not read your other Drive files.

What we store

  • Your CV content (JSON) is stored in your Google Drive, not on our servers.
  • A short-lived sign-in token may be kept in your browser’s sessionStorage until you sign out or it expires.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your CV for advertising.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except Google (for sign-in and Drive storage).

Data deletion

Sign out in the App to clear the local session. To remove your CV file, revoke the App’s access in your Google Account permissions or delete the cv-maker.json file from Google Drive.

Contact

Questions: contact the app owner at the support email listed on the Google OAuth consent screen.
